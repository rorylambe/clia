Steps in the process

How does it work?

Step One

User exports their wearable data

The very first step of the process involves the user exporting their own wearable data via a bespoke app on their iOS or Android device.

Step Two

Data is securely transferred

The wearable health data is then annoymised before being securely transferred to our cloud databank.

Step Three

Data is processed and analysed

Data processing and analytics takes place in our back-end servers and is prepared to be sent to the clinician's software.

Step Four

Clinician views the data

Clinicians and medical professionals can easily view and access their patients' wearable health data through our web-based application.
